Privacy Notice for SellerDataPipeline

Last updated: 13 May 2026

SellerDataPipeline is a private internal data pipeline operated by our company for our own Amazon seller and advertising accounts. The application is not offered to external sellers, advertisers, or the public.

1. Information We Access

With authorization, SellerDataPipeline may access Amazon seller and advertising account data through Amazon Selling Partner API and Amazon Ads API. This may include sales data, finance and settlement data, inventory data, fulfillment data, listing and catalog data, brand analytics data, customer feedback insights, and advertising performance data such as campaign spend, clicks, impressions, attributed sales, ACOS, and ROAS.

2. How We Use the Information

We use this information only for internal business purposes, including operations analysis, advertising performance review, inventory monitoring, financial reconciliation, weekly operations reports, monthly finance reports, and quarterly accounting data packages.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data may be stored in our private cloud database and related storage services. Access is limited to authorized internal users. API credentials and tokens are stored securely and are not committed to public code repositories. We use reasonable technical and organizational controls to protect the information, including access control, encrypted connections, and credential management.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell Amazon data or share raw API credentials with third parties. We may share finance summary reports or quarterly accounting data packages with our external accountant for bookkeeping, tax preparation, and reconciliation purposes. Such reports are intended to exclude Amazon buyer personally identifiable information unless strictly required for a legitimate accounting or compliance purpose.

5. Buyer Personal Information

SellerDataPipeline is designed primarily for business, advertising, finance, inventory, listing, and reporting data. We do not intend to access or store Amazon buyer personally identifiable information unless it is strictly required by an approved Amazon role and business purpose.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary for internal operations, financial reporting, accounting, reconciliation, compliance, and audit purposes.
